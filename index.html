Who We Are

Building a Healthier
South Sudan

Life Nutrics SS Limited is a fully registered company in the Republic of South Sudan, incorporated on 3rd September 2025. Our head office is located at the Nyakuron Cultural Centre, Juba City.

We are aligned with the mission and vision of Global Life Nutrics Sdn. Bhd. in Malaysia and Life Nutrics Africa — committed to promoting health, wealth creation, and improved lifestyles for all.
